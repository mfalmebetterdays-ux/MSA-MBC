Mwasamwanda Well-being Services is a registered firm in Kenya that, aims at facilitating normative development and mental health at individual and group levels across the lifespan through effective professional, evidence-based and innovative psychological interventions. Its services which entail consultancy and advisory, counselling and psychotherapy, and training are based on current global professional standards and trends to enable people function as expected across the lifespan hence lead a fulfilling and happy life. The firm has competent and experienced psychologists ready to offer professional and reliable services. Our services are affordable and accessible hence offered through in person, telephone and online modes. Read more
